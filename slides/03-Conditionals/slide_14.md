# Comparison operators

A **comparison operator** compares its operands and evaluates to a boolean value based on whether or not the comparison is true.

## Comparison Operators Table

| Operator | Name |
|----------|------|
| < | less than |
| <= | less than or equal to |
| > | greater than |
| >= | greater than or equal to |
| === | equal to |
| !== | not equal to |
