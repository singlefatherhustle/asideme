# Adjacency list

The space complexity of an adjacency list is O(V+E), where V is the number of vertices and E is the number of edges.

## What are the time complexities of:

- checking if an edge exists? **O(E)**
- getting the weight of an edge? **O(E)**
- adding an edge? **O(1)**
- removing an edge? **O(E)**
- adding a vertex? **O(1)**
- removing a vertex? **O(V+E)**
- getting all neighbors of a vertex? **O(E)**
