# Adjacency matrix

The space complexity of an adjacency matrix is O(V²), where V is the number of vertices.

What are the time complexities of:
- checking if an edge exists?
- getting the weight of an edge?
- adding or removing an edge?
- adding or removing a vertex?
- getting all neighbors of a vertex?
