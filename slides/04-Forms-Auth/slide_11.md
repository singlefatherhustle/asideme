# Verify your identity

The receptionist looks at your ID and checks the system to confirm your reservation. Everything looks good, so they hand you a hotel keycard.

## Diagram

```
You ←─── keycard ───┬─── Front Desk ───checks──→ system
                    │
                    └─ "Enjoy your stay!"
```

### Components:
- **You** (stick figure on left)
- **keycard** (hotel key icon in center)
- **Front Desk** (stick figure in center-right)
- **system** (computer monitor and keyboard on right)

### Flow:
1. You receive keycard from Front Desk
2. Front Desk checks the system
3. System confirms reservation
4. Front Desk says "Enjoy your stay!"
