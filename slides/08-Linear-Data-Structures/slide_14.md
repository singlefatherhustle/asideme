# Data structures & algorithms

The same data can be stored in different data structures, leading to different time and space complexities for the same operations.

In other words, different *data structures* enable different *algorithms*, and that is why they are taught together.

The most important skill to learn in this unit is the ability to choose the best approach by analyzing the trade-offs between different potential solutions.
