# For loops

A **for loop** is *syntactic sugar* for a while loop. They do the exact same thing!

## Comparison

| While Loop | For Loop |
|---|---|
| `let i = 0;`<br>`while (i < 3){`<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;`console.log("Counting..." + i);`<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;`i++;`<br>`}` | `for (let i = 0; i < 3; i++){`<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;`console.log("Counting..." + i);`<br>`}` |
