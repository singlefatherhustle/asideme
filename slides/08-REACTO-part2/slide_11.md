# How do you optimize?

If you have a working solution, how do you make it better? There's no single magic answer, unfortunately, but you can start by asking yourself these questions:

- Is there information in the problem that was left unused?
- Is this problem similar to previous problems you've solved before?
- Where are the bottlenecks in your current solution?
- Are there better data structures or algorithms you could use?
