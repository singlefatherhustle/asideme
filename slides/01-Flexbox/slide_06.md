# Grid item

By default, grid containers will automatically place its direct children, called **grid items**, into individual cells, starting from the top left cell.

## Grid Layout Diagram

A 5-column by 4-row grid structure showing:

| Column | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
|--------|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Row 1** | A | B | C | (empty) | (empty) |
| **Row 2** | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) |
| **Row 3** | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) |
| **Row 4** | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) | (empty) |

**Grid Items shown:**
- Item A: positioned in column 1, row 1
- Item B: positioned in column 2, row 1
- Item C: positioned in column 3, row 1
- Columns 4-5 in row 1 are empty/highlighted in light yellow
- Rows 2-4 are empty/highlighted in light yellow
