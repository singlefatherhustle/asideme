# Recap

- A **foreign key** constraint enforces **referential integrity**.
- **Cascade** operations to related records to avoid violating referential integrity.
- A foreign key with *no* other constraints represents an *optional* **1-N** relationship.
- A foreign key with a NOT NULL constraint represents a *mandatory* **1-N** relationship.
- A foreign key with a UNIQUE constraint represents a **1-1** relationship.
