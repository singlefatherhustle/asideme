# Controlled vs uncontrolled

These two terms are used to describe whether a component is mostly driven by its own state, or by props passed down from its parent.

A **controlled** component does not have its own state. Instead, its behavior is *controlled* by its parent through props.

An **uncontrolled** component has its own local state which *cannot* be controlled by its parent.
