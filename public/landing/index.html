Questions

The honest answers.

Where does the audio go?

Audio streams to a speech-to-text service for the few seconds it takes to turn into text — then the audio itself is discarded. Only the transcript stays, in your account's private database. The AI that answers your questions sees only the question text plus the small relevant excerpt of the transcript — never the audio. Full details on the sub-processors page.

Does it really work offline?

Reading does. Once you've loaded the library, every cached doc is available with the network off. Generating new summaries, quizzes, or flashcards needs a connection because that's what calls the AI.

Can I install it on my phone?

Yes. ASIDE is a PWA. On iOS Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android Chrome: the install prompt appears automatically once you've loaded the page. The installed app gets its own icon, runs in a standalone window, and respects the system theme.

What AI is answering my questions?

Anthropic's Claude — a top-tier large language model. Different sizes of the model are used for different tasks (faster ones for quizzes and summaries, the stronger one for chat answers). Full details on the sub-processors page.

Who is this for?

Anyone in a class or licensing prep who has felt that "I missed the last sentence and can't ask again" feeling — coding bootcamp students, real-estate and contractor exam candidates, HVAC and plumbing trade students, nursing review attendees, CPA and bar prep, anyone studying for a certification. Bring your own materials and ASIDE shapes itself to your subject.

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?

ASIDE is grounded in the actual transcript of your class — not the internet. Questions retrieve real lines your instructor said, so answers reference what was actually taught, not what a foundation model thinks the topic is "usually" about.
