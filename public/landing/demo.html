How it works

A tutor that remembers every line of the lecture.

Audio in, transcript out, an answer on call. Bring your own study materials and ASIDE shapes itself to whatever subject you're certifying in. Ask anything that was just said and it points back to the exact moment in the lecture.

01

Listen.

One click captures your live class. Built-in mic, system audio via BlackHole, or both at once.

02

Transcribe.

Speech-to-text streams in real time. Every word is searchable as it lands.

03

Ask.

Type or speak a question. ASIDE answers using the part of the lecture that just happened — no hallucination, no guessing.

04

Study.

Generate quizzes, flashcards, and summaries from any topic. Export to Markdown or PDF.

05

Upload.

Drop in whatever you're studying — exam handbooks, MLS references, building codes, lecture slides, nursing protocols, statute books. ASIDE indexes your materials alongside the live transcript so every answer cites your source of truth, not a generic one.

06

Own.

Audio is never stored — only the transcript is, and it lives in a local SQLite file on your machine. Works offline once you've loaded the docs. Delete everything any time.
