🎙 Before you record

ASIDE is about to start capturing audio from your microphone or system. You are responsible for getting consent from everyone whose voice is being recorded.

⚠ Two-party-consent states

In CA, CT, DE, FL, IL, MD, MA, MI, MT, NV, NH, OR, PA, VT, WA (and the EU/UK), recording any conversation without every participant's consent can be a criminal offense — including the instructor and other students in your class.

Ask first. If you're not sure, don't record. ASIDE always shows a visible recording indicator while the mic is hot, but the disclosure to others is on you.
