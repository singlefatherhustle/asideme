# Background Check / Fingerprinting

## What's required

All Georgia real estate license applicants must submit to:

- LiveScan fingerprinting
- FBI / state criminal background check
- Application review by GREC

## LiveScan process

```
1. Find an approved LiveScan vendor (search "LiveScan Georgia" online)
2. Schedule an appointment (often same-day or next-day)
3. Pay vendor fee (~$60-80)
4. Take government-issued photo ID
5. Get fingerprinted electronically
6. Vendor submits to GBI and FBI
7. Results forwarded to GREC
```

## What disqualifies

GREC reviews any criminal history. Specific disqualifiers:

### Automatic disqualifications

- Felony involving fraud, theft, or dishonesty (within recent years)
- Sexual offense involving minor
- Felony violation of real estate license law in any state
- License revocation in another state still pending

### Case-by-case review

- Misdemeanors involving moral turpitude
- DUI / DWI (older convictions less weight)
- Domestic violence
- Drug offenses (depends on substance and disposition)
- Mental health commitments

### Generally not disqualifying

- Traffic violations (unless involving DUI)
- Old convictions (10+ years) that have been expunged
- Disposition of "not guilty" or dismissal
- Pardoned offenses

## The "good moral character" standard

Georgia license law requires applicants to demonstrate "good moral character." GREC interprets this case-by-case:

- Was the offense recent or remote?
- Has the person rehabilitated?
- Is the offense related to real estate practice?
- What was the disposition?
- Has the person made restitution if applicable?

## Restoration / pre-approval

If you have a criminal record and want to know your status BEFORE paying application fees:

1. Contact GREC and request a "fitness review"
2. Submit documentation of disposition + rehabilitation evidence
3. GREC issues a written opinion on whether they would issue a license
4. This pre-approval doesn't bind GREC at full application but provides direction

## Costs

```
LiveScan fingerprinting: $60-80
Background check fee: included or separate $30-60
Total: $90-140
```

## Common background check errors

- Skipping the LiveScan entirely (application returned)
- Using non-approved vendor
- Failing to disclose criminal history on application (perjury — automatic disqualifier)
- Not addressing the disposition of a charge (need full court documents)
- Waiting too long after pre-license course (background results may need to be re-run)

## Processing time

- LiveScan submission to GBI: 1-2 business days
- GBI to FBI: 1-3 weeks
- Results to GREC: 1 week
- GREC application review: 2-4 weeks

Total: 1-2 months from LiveScan to license issuance. Plan accordingly.

## Privacy

Background check results:

- Are confidential between GREC and you
- Not posted publicly unless disciplinary action results
- May be subpoenaed in litigation, but generally protected
- Not available to your sponsoring broker without your consent
