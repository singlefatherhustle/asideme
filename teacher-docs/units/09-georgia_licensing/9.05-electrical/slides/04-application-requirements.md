# Application Requirements

## The mandatory checklist

Every Georgia electrical contractor application must include all of the following. Missing one is an automatic deficiency notice from the board:

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Age** | At least **21 years old** at time of application |
| **Application form** | Current Division 553 form, fully completed |
| **References** | Three (3), at least one from a licensed electrical contractor |
| **Experience** | Four (4) years of primary electrical experience documented |
| **Exam fee** | Currently $98 (PSI exam fee, subject to change) |
| **Application fee** | Currently $30 to the board |
| **Photo ID** | Government-issued, used at testing center |

## What "primary" experience means

The board distinguishes:

- **Primary experience** — actual hands-on installation, repair, or maintenance of electrical systems under a licensed contractor. Counts at 100%.
- **Secondary experience** — engineering design, electrical inspection, electrical estimating, electrical instruction, or related supervisory work where you did not install. Counts at **50%**.

Both contribute to the 4-year total, but secondary experience is capped at **one year** of credit.

## Reference letter format

Each reference letter should:

1. Be on letterhead (preferred) or include the writer's full mailing address
2. State **how long** the writer has known the applicant
3. State **what capacity** (employer, foreman, peer)
4. Affirm the applicant's character and competency
5. Include a contact phone number the board can verify

## Pre-flight checklist

Before submitting:

- [ ] All 4 years of experience itemized by employer + dates
- [ ] At least one reference is from a Georgia-licensed electrical contractor
- [ ] Application is signed AND notarized
- [ ] Fees made out correctly (split between PSI and the board)
- [ ] Photo ID copy attached

## Common rejection reasons

- Experience gaps the applicant didn't account for (e.g., "I worked there but they closed")
- Secondary experience claimed at 100% instead of 50%
- Missing licensed-contractor reference
- Applicant under 21
- Notary omitted
