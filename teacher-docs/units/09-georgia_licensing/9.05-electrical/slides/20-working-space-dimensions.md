# Working Space Dimensions (NEC 110.26)

## The rule everyone tests on

NEC 110.26(A)(1) defines minimum working space around equipment likely to require examination, adjustment, servicing, or maintenance while energized.

## Depth of working space — Table 110.26(A)(1)

Depth required depends on:
1. **Nominal voltage to ground**
2. **Condition** (1, 2, or 3) — what's on the opposite side

| Voltage to ground | Cond. 1 | Cond. 2 | Cond. 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–150 V | 3 ft | 3 ft | 3 ft |
| 151–600 V | 3 ft | 3-1/2 ft | 4 ft |
| 601 V – 2.5 kV | 3 ft | 4 ft | 5 ft |
| 2.5–9 kV | 4 ft | 5 ft | 6 ft |

**Conditions:**
- **Condition 1** — Exposed live parts on one side, NO live or grounded parts on the other (or insulated wire/cable)
- **Condition 2** — Exposed live parts on one side, grounded parts on the other (concrete walls count as grounded)
- **Condition 3** — Exposed live parts on both sides

## Width

The width of working space must be the width of the equipment or **30 inches**, whichever is greater. The 30-inch width must permit at least a 90° door opening.

## Height — 110.26(A)(3)

Working space must be clear from the floor (or grade) up to the height of the equipment or **6-1/2 feet**, whichever is greater.

Within the working space, no equipment foreign to the electrical installation may be located above OR below the electrical equipment, except:

- Sprinkler protection piping that crosses overhead is allowed
- Suspended ceilings less than 6 ft above the floor are not allowed in the workspace

## Dedicated equipment space — 110.26(E)

The space EQUAL to the width and depth of the equipment, extending UP to 6 ft above the equipment (or to the structural ceiling, whichever is lower), must be dedicated to the electrical installation. No piping, ducts, equipment foreign to the electrical work.

```
    ┌──────────────────────┐
    │  6 ft dedicated      │   no foreign piping, ducts
    │  space above         │
    ├──────────────────────┤
    │      EQUIPMENT       │
    └──────────────────────┘
    Floor
```
