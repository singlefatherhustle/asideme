# Financial Responsibility and Insurance

## Class I requirements

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Financial responsibility** | $25,000 by listed proof method |
| **General liability insurance** | $300,000 minimum |
| **Workers' compensation** | If required by employee count |

## Class II requirements

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Financial responsibility** | $25,000 by listed proof method |
| **General liability insurance** | $500,000 minimum |
| **Workers' compensation** | If required by employee count |

## Proof methods for $25,000 financial responsibility

The board accepts these proofs:

1. **Surety bond** of $25,000 from approved surety
2. **Letter of credit** for $25,000 from approved financial institution
3. **Cash deposit** of $25,000 with the board
4. **Audited financial statement** showing net worth ≥ $25,000

Most contractors use a surety bond — it's the cheapest annual cost (~$250-500/yr depending on credit).

## General liability insurance

Required minimum coverage:

```
Class I:  $300,000 per occurrence
Class II: $500,000 per occurrence
```

This covers third-party bodily injury and property damage caused by the contractor's work. NOT the same as:

- Workers' compensation (employee injury — separate policy)
- Errors & omissions (professional liability — separate)
- Property insurance for the contractor's own assets

## Workers' compensation

Required when:

- Georgia: 3 or more employees (including contractors)
- Sole proprietorship with no employees: not required for self
- Subcontractors: may need their own coverage

Failing to carry workers' comp when required:

- License suspension
- Criminal penalty
- Personal liability for employee injuries

## Insurance certificates

Each insurance policy must:

- Be from an approved carrier
- Name the contractor's licensed business
- Be current (not expired)
- Be submitted to GREC with each license renewal

## Common financial responsibility errors

- Letting surety bond lapse (license suspended)
- Failing to maintain GL insurance coverage
- Mixing personal and business insurance
- Working without workers' comp when required
- Insurance certificate not updated after coverage changes

## Cost summary

```
Surety bond (annual): $250-500
GL insurance Class I (annual): $1,200-2,000
GL insurance Class II (annual): $2,000-4,000
Workers' comp (annual, varies by payroll): $1,000-5,000+
Total Class I annual insurance: $2,500-5,000
Total Class II annual insurance: $4,000-10,000
```

These are operating costs, not optional. Build them into business pricing.
